About Us



What we do




Our mission is to build a thriving community of École Polytechnique Bachelor students and alumni who are either pursuing or working in finance, whether in quantitative finance, investment banking, or other finance-related fields.

With the support of our partners and sponsors, we equip students with the skills needed to succeed in finance through online courses, interview preparation, and local competitions, including those organized by our partners.

Our experienced members share insights from their interview experiences and the admissions processes to help guide younger members into the financial world.

We also aim to keep our community informed about the latest developments in finance through regular newsletters and meetings.

Whether you're interested in quantitative finance or finance in general, join us and take the next step in your journey!

The Finance University Network (FUN)

As a member of the FUN, each university contributes to shaping the future of global finance by participating in research initiatives, exclusive events, and networking opportunities. Contact us if you want to join.

IRL - Trinity College Dublin

Trinity College Dublin is renowned for its innovative finance programs and active global research contributions.

As a member of the FUN, it collaborates with financial leaders worldwide.

FRA - Essec Business School

Essec Business School excels in finance education, offering top-tier programs in investment banking, risk management, and more.

Essec provides students hands-on experience in the global finance ecosystem.

UK - London School of Economics

The London School of Economics (LSE) is a crucial hub in the UK's finance network, fostering collaboration among scholars and and professionals. Its societies and connections enhance knowledge exchange, driving innovation and leadership in finance and quantitative research.
